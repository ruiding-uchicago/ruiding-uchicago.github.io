<div class="section-header">
    <h1 class="section-title">Research Directions</h1>
</div>

<p>Traditional materials discovery is slow and expensive. Standard ML approaches remain fragmented. I'm building integrated AI systems that can autonomously navigate the full research cycle.</p>

<div class="section-header mt-8">
    <h2 class="section-title">AI Agents for Autonomous Discovery</h2>
</div>

<p>Research agents that can:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Conduct deep literature review with local RAG systems</li>
    <li>Generate and prioritize research hypotheses</li>
    <li>Design experiments through active learning</li>
    <li>Interpret results and iterate autonomously</li>
</ul>

<p>Our <strong>Deep Tree of Research (DToR)</strong> agent achieved ~79% win rate against commercial deep-research systems across 27 nanomaterials topics, running entirely on local consumer hardware.</p>

<div class="section-header mt-8">
    <h2 class="section-title">Cross-Modal Materials Intelligence</h2>
</div>

<p>Unifying heterogeneous scientific data:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Text from papers, patents, and databases</li>
    <li>Molecular graphs and crystal structures</li>
    <li>Microscopy images and spectra</li>
    <li>Experimental measurements and simulations</li>
</ul>

<p>Goal: Enable AI systems to reason across modalities the way human researchers do.</p>
